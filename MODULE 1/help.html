Help & FAQs

📝 How to Register for Events

  1. Visit the Registration section on the homepage.
  2. Enter your personal information and choose an event.
  3. Click the "Register" button to submit your details.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email shortly.

🗣️ Submitting Feedback

  1. Scroll down to the Feedback section.
  2. Select the event you attended.
  3. Rate your experience and add comments.
  4. Click the "Submit Feedback" button to share your thoughts.

🛠️ Technical Issues

  1. Try refreshing the page or reopening your browser.
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  3. Switch to a different browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox).
  4. Contact our support team if problems persist.
